Subscription Management

To manage, cancel, or restore your DaTrack Pro subscription, go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored on a server?
No. All your expense data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

How does AI work in DaTrack?
When you type an expense or scan a receipt, the text or image is sent to Google Gemini for real-time processing. No data is stored externally.

Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Settings and tap Export to download all your expenses as a CSV file.
